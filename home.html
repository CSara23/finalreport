Team Retrospect

This week, the team had a great sprint. Stories were fledged out and the user journeys helped to make implementation easier.

Development wise, the roles are now clearer. Some members of the team will focus on technical implementation, whilst others will focus on requirement analysis and UI design.

Moving forward, it is anticipated that this will help the team work faster due to clarity.

The core challenge face this week, technically was form validation. This was a piece of work that was pull into the sprint as the team had capacity. The team did a spike that included reviewing review the golang documentation and thus the output will be used to implement the validations in the next sprint.

Another challenge was communication. The team needs to communicate better so that efforts will not be wasted
